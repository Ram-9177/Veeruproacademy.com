Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please read our refund policy carefully.

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all course purchases. If you're not satisfied with your course, you can request a full refund within 7 days of purchase.

Eligibility for Refund

To be eligible for a refund:

  • Request must be made within 7 days of purchase
  • You must not have completed more than 30% of the course
  • You must not have downloaded course materials
  • No certificate of completion has been issued

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Email us at support@veeruproacademy.com
  2. Include your order ID and reason for refund
  3. We will process your request within 5-7 business days
  4. Refunds will be credited to the original payment method

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Courses purchased during promotional sales
  • Bundle packages (partial refunds may apply)
  • Courses with downloadable content already accessed

Questions?

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact our support team.
